THE TOY STORE - VENDOR & SUPPLIER FAQ

Q: How do I become an approved vendor for The Toy Store?
A: All prospective vendors must submit a formal application through our Vendor Portal. Applications are reviewed quarterly. You must provide proof of product safety certifications (CPSC or EN71 standards) and carry a minimum of $1,000,000 in product liability insurance.

Q: What are your standard payment terms?
A: Our standard payment terms for new vendors are Net 60 days. After 6 months of successful, on-time deliveries without major defect issues, this can be renegotiated to Net 30 days upon request.

Q: How are warehouse deliveries scheduled?
A: Deliveries must be scheduled at least 48 hours in advance using our Warehouse Management System. Our primary receiving hours are Monday through Friday, 8:00 AM to 3:00 PM. Unscheduled deliveries may be turned away or subject to a holding fee.

Q: What are the requirements for product packaging?
A: All master cartons must be clearly labeled with our internal SKU, quantity, weight, and a scannable UPC/EAN barcode. Individual items must be retail-ready. We do not accept loose inventory.

Q: How do you handle vendor-defective merchandise (RTV)?
A: Defective merchandise reported by customers within the first 90 days will be aggregated and Returned to Vendor (RTV) on a monthly basis. The cost of defective goods plus return freight will be deducted from the vendor's next invoice.
