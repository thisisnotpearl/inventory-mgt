THE TOY STORE - RETURN AND REFUND POLICY
Effective Date: January 1, 2026

1. GENERAL RETURNS
Customers may return any unopened, unused item within 30 days of the original purchase date for a full refund to the original payment method. The item must be in its original packaging with all factory seals intact. A receipt or proof of purchase is required for all returns.

2. OPENED ITEMS
If an item has been opened but is otherwise in "like new" condition with all parts and manuals included, it can be returned within 14 days of purchase. However, these returns are subject to a 15% restocking fee, and the refund will be issued as store credit only.

3. DAMAGED OR DEFECTIVE ITEMS
If you receive a toy that is damaged in transit or defective out of the box, you must report it to our customer service team within 48 hours of delivery. We will provide a pre-paid return shipping label and issue a full refund or send a replacement immediately upon receiving the defective item. Do not attempt to repair defective electronic toys, as this voids the warranty.

4. NON-RETURNABLE ITEMS
The following items are strictly non-returnable:
- Clearance items (marked down by 50% or more)
- Blind bags, mystery boxes, or trading card packs that have been opened
- Personalized or custom-made items
- Gift cards

5. HOLIDAY RETURN POLICY
Any items purchased between November 1st and December 24th may be returned until January 31st of the following year, provided they meet the conditions outlined in Section 1 or 2.
